Fishy

The rate of commercial fish farming in India is steadily increasing. Because there is a high demand for fish and fish products in the Indian market. And, in line with current population growth, the consumption rate and demand for fish are gradually increasing. The sea and rivers are the primary and most important sources of fish. However, due to the enormous volume of fish collected, the amount of fish from those natural resources is steadily decreasing. As a result, establishing a commercial fish farming enterprise is essential in order to meet the nutritional needs of the Indian population. India is endowed with a plethora of rivers, lakes, and other natural water resources.
